HISTORY, VISION & MISSION

Of Our Sarhad CBSE School

VISION

Enabling all learners to recognize their worth, to foster creative thinking, and to play an active role in society, in its many facets. "We picture education not simply as an acquisition of knowledge, but rather as an experience, which teaches all people how to grow and think."

MISSION

"Our goal is to spread a love of learning by creating an atmosphere where everyone's voice is valued and inquiry is encouraged. Our goal is to develop creative thinkers who welcome challenges and pursue knowledge outside of the classroom. Our goal is to establish a thriving ecosystem that promotes innovation, diversity, and resilience through cooperative relationships with families and the community. We give students the tools they need to become critical thinkers and compassionate leaders who can successfully negotiate the challenges of the real world and contribute significantly to society."

Core Values:
  • Integrity: The single most important core value that enables choosing between right and wrong and standing for what is right at all times.
  • Dedication: Wholehearted commitment to the cause that one has identified oneself with.
  • Discipline: In thoughts and actions that lead to fearlessness.
  • Team Spirit: Enables working in perfect harmony with colleagues and teammates to achieve the desired synergy.
