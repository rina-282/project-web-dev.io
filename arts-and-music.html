ART & MUSIC

Of Our Sarhad CBSE School




Painting

Our students explore various painting techniques, expressing creativity and emotions on canvas.

Sculpture

Sculpting fosters artistic skills and patience, turning raw materials into beautiful works of art.

Drawing

Drawing is a fundamental art skill, helping students to refine their creativity and visual thinking.

Instruments

Music education includes learning to play a variety of instruments, fostering discipline and creativity.

Choir

Our school choir brings students together to create beautiful music through harmony and collaboration.

Dance

Dance is an art form that expresses emotions through movement, helping students develop coordination and rhythm.
