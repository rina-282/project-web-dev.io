about school

It was established in 2004 and is located at Katraj in Pune. It caters to all who expect quality education for their wards with Indian value system. Sarhad School sets itself apart through thoughtful learning environment and our unwavering commitment to addressing your child’s needs at every step of their educational journey. With about 2 decades of experience, Sarhad School has emerged as a trusted one for holistic development. We at Sarhad School try to –
• Provide a positive, caring, and engaging learning environment.
• Provide children with the opportunity to establish real-world connections.
• Provide the joy of experiencing exploration, discovery, and developing creativity during each step of their educational journey.
• Fosters independent learners
