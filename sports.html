SPORTS

Of Our Sarhad CBSE School

Cricket

Cricket is a team sport played with a bat and ball, enjoyed by students of all ages.

Football

Football is a popular team sport that encourages teamwork, fitness, and coordination.

Basketball

Basketball is a fast-paced game that improves agility and teamwork.

Volleyball

Volleyball promotes hand-eye coordination and is a fantastic team sport.

Badminton

Badminton is a racket sport that improves reflexes and concentration.

Table Tennis

Table Tennis is an exciting indoor sport that enhances quick reflexes.
